Folder Structure

Here is the folder structure we follow for our project:

  • /app
    • /images
      • /content
      • /interface
    • /javascript
      • /data
      • /handlebars
      • /modules
        • exampleModule.js
      • application.js
      • config.js
    • /stylesheets
      • /blocks
        • block-name.sass
      • /global
        • buttons.sass
        • typography.sass
      • /templates
        • homepage.sass
      • /tools
        • /bourbon
        • /functions
        • /mixins
      • /variables
        • colors.sass
        • fonts.sass
    • /vendor
